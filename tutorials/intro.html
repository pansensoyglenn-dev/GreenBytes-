The Architectural Shift

The essence of OOP in the era of huge data and non-deterministic AI.

Huge Data Scaling

Procedural logic collapses under the weight of petabyte-scale data streams. OOP provides the containment required to prevent "data bleed"—ensuring that massive state changes remain isolated and predictable.

AI Determinism

In 2026, AI agents generate logic on the fly. OOP classes act as the "Laws of Physics" for these agents, defining strict boundaries that even the most advanced LLMs cannot bypass during execution.
