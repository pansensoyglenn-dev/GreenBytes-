Cookie Policy

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and store certain information about your preferences or past actions.

How GreenBytes Uses Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience and to help us understand how our tutorials are being used. Specifically, we use them for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics: To monitor traffic patterns and improve our educational content.
  • Advertising: Third-party vendors like Google use cookies to serve ads that are relevant to your interests.

Managing Your Preferences

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can choose to block all cookies or receive a notification before a cookie is set. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of this website.
